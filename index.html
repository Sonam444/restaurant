About Us

Sonam's Buffet Paradise, led by renowned Michelin star chef Sonam Tamang, is an epitome of culinary excellence. With a rich history of gastronomic innovation, Sonam has earned numerous prestigious awards and accolades, including the Michelin Star, Golden Chef's Hat, and International Culinary Excellence Award. Our restaurant boasts a warm and welcoming atmosphere, where guests are treated to impeccable service and unforgettable dining experiences. Committed to maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and cleanliness, we ensure a safe and enjoyable environment for all our patrons. Come and indulge in a gastronomic journey like no other at Sonam's Buffet Paradise.
